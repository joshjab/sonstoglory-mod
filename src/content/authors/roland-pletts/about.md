Roland received Jesus in the mid 1960s and over a period of about six months came to an assurance of salvation, was baptised and filled with the Spirit. He went into ministry as a young man and saw amazing events in Africa: people delivered from demons, healings, the conversion of a "witch doctor", great outpourings of the Holy Spirit, the death of martyrs, miraculous deliverance from harm and death, words of knowledge and gifts of prophecy. He endured a seven year war and ministered to people of all faiths and walks of life. He then worked in churches and missions in Central Africa for four decades. His eight books on this website come from nearly five decades of Biblical study. Roland and his wife currently live in the United Kingdom.
