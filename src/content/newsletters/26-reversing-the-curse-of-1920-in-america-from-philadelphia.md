# Reversing the Curse of 1920

by Paul Jablonowski

August 5, 2016


<img src="/images/newsletters/pictures/sandy%20hook%20bay.jpg" data-align="left" data-vspace="8" data-hspace="8" width="406" height="325" />I just finished reading a book called "The Curse of 1920"(1). It grieved my heart to read how there may have been a three-pronged curse initiated over America in 1920 using the: 1) women's rights extremists (feminist movement), 2) voodoo Zimran music (jazz, rock and rap), and 3) abortion and euthanasia. All of these began in earnest in the year of 1920 and continued to get worse over the years. So, I was sighing and crying over the abominations (Ezek. 9:4) which are being committed in my nation of America.

The next day, I was scheduled to travel for my current engineering job from Atlanta to Philadelphia and staying in New Jersey. When planning my travel, I noticed a map of Sandy Hook Bay in northern NJ that happened to have a pitchfork shaped dock in the bay which is pointed towards the Statue of Liberty and Brooklyn NY. I sensed this was symbolic of the three pronged curse. So I contacted my friend Jane who lives in Long Island NY and it led to a three day time of intercession where we prayed together over the phone in three key locations.

These three locations were: 1) the RCA building in Camden NJ, 2) the Liberty Bell in Philadelphia, and 3) the Wells Fargo Center in Philadelphia. Each of these represented one of the prongs in the three pronged curse that we were praying to be reversed in our nation. This is called identificational intercession which is how Yeshua (Jesus) intercedes for us, by identifying with our sins. Yahweh did not just sympathize with us in our sins, but He came to this earth as a man in Yeshua to identify with us and provide an atonement for our sins.

I was traveling the Atlanta subways and airport systems at the exact time Obama was traveling in Atlanta from 1-4 pm. He was speaking at a political event since we are only 3 months away from presidential elections. Obama's travel shut down the Atlanta airport for a short period of time, but Atlanta is the busiest airport in the world, so it effects a lot of people. The airports and public transports are "gateways" in cities that can be used for both good and evil, and as intercessors we are called to keep these gates open for the Kingdom of Yahweh and shut them from evil. It was here during my delay in Atlanta that I began to realize my business travel was about to turn into a time of serious intercession and identificational repentance for our nation in regards to the three prong curse of 1920.

## 1) VICTOR RCA building

On Tuesday my job happened to schedule a dinner for us at a historic building in Camden NJ called the VICTOR which is the old restored RCA building where much of modern day rock music had its beginning. After my dinner, I circled this building in prayer and sounded my shofar. Then Jane and I prayed together on the phone in regards to reversing the curse upon this music and restoring Yahweh honoring music. There were many homeless people walking around because this section of town used to be very bad, but they have started a renovation project here to restore it and make it a nicer place for people to visit. I believe this is the middle prong of the pitchfork that we were repenting of regarding voodoo Zimran music.

## 2) Liberty Bell

After work on Wednesday, I went to the Liberty Bell in downtown Philadelphia where Jane and I prayed together over the phone for Yahweh to release TRUE liberty over the land. There were many children walking around the liberty bell which I believe represents the third prong of the curse of 1920 (abortion & euthanasia) that is now starting to be reversed. There are statistics coming out that abortion in America has started to go down during Obama's presidency(2), which was prayed for in my <a href="02AtonementCivilRightsfortheUnborn.htm" target="_blank">2008 newsletter</a>. I also walked around Independence Hall where the Declaration of Independence was signed and blew my shofar, praying for the modern day <a href="../covenantofpeace.htm" target="_blank">Phinehas'</a> to come forth bringing true lasting peace!

## 3) Wells Fargo Center

Thursday, on my way to the Philadelphia airport, I stopped at the Wells Fargo Center where the Democratic National Convention was held just last week. There were still many workers there cleaning up things and the signs of the convention were still all over the city and this building. I called Jane and this was the most special part of the intercession because she was identifying with Hillary Clinton saying she was born within a year of Hillary and attended some of the early feminist movement meetings back in the 1960's. However, she could not join in with them knowing it was a wrong spirit. At this point Jane had read several chapters in this book, and was in agreement that the feminists movement was indeed a curse on our nation and prayed with me to reverse this curse! I blew the shofar standing in front of this building and Jane agreed with me for Yahweh to begin reversing this curse by not allowing Hillary to get into the presidency as it would only further these type curses. I believe this curse is continuing to be reversed through these types of prayers and intercessions as we cry out and decree to the courtrooms of heaven to render a verdict based on the forgiving blood of Jesus!
