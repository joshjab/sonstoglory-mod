# Bible Supernaturally Producing Oil

Paul Jablonowski

December 20, 2017


Jerry Pearce in Dalton Georgia has a Bible that is supernaturally producing oil!(1)

It started in Psalm 39 of his personal bible with just a few oily spots that he thought his grand child had spilled on a few pages of his Bible. Then within a few days it spread from Psalm 39 through Psalm 63, and proceeded to fill his Bible with oil from Psalms to Revelation. Then, the oil started in the first book of Genesis and saturated it to Psalms until the entire bible was full of oil.

This all started in late January of 2017, and within a few weeks the bible was put into a clear plastic container because it was producing so much oil. Now it sits in a 5 gallon clear plastic container which ebbs and flows in oil level as they fill oil vials and send them all over the world. Jerry told me that they have mailed and given out tens of thousands of these small oil vials. He also said the LORD told them that as long as they continue to give the oil away for free without any cost that it will continue to flow.

<img src="/images/newsletters/pictures/oil_vial_from_Dalton_GA.jpg" data-align="left" data-vspace="8" data-hspace="8" width="200" height="274" />Pictured here is the oil and vial that they gave me when I met them. The main purpose of the oil is to use it to pray for others according to James 5:14-15 - *"Is anyone among you sick? Let him call for the elders of the church, and let them pray over him, **anointing him with oil** in the name of the Lord. And the prayer of faith will save the sick, and the Lord will raise him up. And if he has committed sins, he will be forgiven."* (James 5:14-15) So the goal is to bless others by praying healing for them. And there have been many testimonies of people being supernaturally healed in these gatherings. However, when Jesus did most of His healing miracles he told them **not to tell anyone!** - see Matt 8:4, Mark 7:36, Luke 8:56 -

I think it is significant that the oil started flowing from Psalm 39 (A Psalm of David) part of which says:

*"I said I will guard my ways, Lest I sin with my tongue; **I will restrain my mouth with a muzzle**, while the wicked are before me.
**I was mute with silence, I held my peace even from good**; And my sorrow was stirred up.
My heart was hot within me; While I was meditating, the fire burned.
Then I spoke with my tongue:
LORD make me to know my end, And what is the measure of my days, That I may know how frail I am.
Indeed, You have made my days as handbreadths, And my age is as nothing before you; Certainly every man at his best state is but vapor. Selah

Surely every man walks about like a shadow; Surely they busy themselves in vain; He heaps up riches, And does not know who will gather them.
And now, Lord, what do I wait for? My hope is in You. Deliver me from all my transgressions; Do not make me the reproach of the foolish.
**I was mute, I did not open my mouth**, Because it was You who did it."* (Psalm 39:1-9)

There is something special about **being silent** in the presence of the LORD. And this oil is about the presence of the LORD. I have attended two Tuesday morning prayer meetings at the "Grace251 gift shop" in Dalton Georgia with the bible and oil bucket, and the precious sweet presence of the LORD is most definitely there! December 05, 2017 was the first time I visited with my friend Miles and met Johnny Taylor who preaches in their videos. December 19, 2017 was the second time I visited with my friend Eugene and talked to Jerry Pearce who owns the oil producing bible. There is much worship in song at these meetings; and I personally saw the oil grow in the container without anyone "adding" anything to it during our several hour long visit.

I believe these brethren are humble stewards of this sign and wonder. Larger ministries have offered them a stage but it included marketing and charging money for the oil and so they refused to accept it.

My second visit to see the oil was during Hanukkah which is also called the Feast of Dedication in John 10:22 that Jesus celebrated. Hanukkah is a memorial of the re-dedication of the temple about 140 years before Jesus' time. The miracle which happened at the first Hanukkah was the supernatural reproduction of oil! Jewish history says that one day's supply of oil for the Menorah lasted for eight days! So, miraculous oil is for the purpose of RESTORATION! This includes restoring the temples of our bodies (physical healing) and restoring God ordained relationships, purposes and destinies.

This supernatural oil being produced from a brother's bible has been tested in a lab. The lab said that it is similar to a mineral oil, but its not a mineral oil and there is nothing like it in the earth. The oil is very clear and thick too, but I personally cannot smell anything from it. It does have very unique properties and even burns on a wick like oil in a lamp.

Interestingly, an ancient clay lamp was found in Israel just before Hanukkah this year. A seven year old girl walking with her mom found this lamp near Beit Shean Israel and it has been dated by the Israeli Antiquities to be around the time of the first Hanukkah. They believe it was unearthed by a porcupine who was digging out his home near an archeological site.(2) The little girl who found this lamp is named Hadas which was Esther's original name; and Esther met the king for the first time during the dates of Hanukkah! (Esther 2:16). Also, "Kibbutz Nir David" where the lamp was found means "Lamp of David." I believe the LORD's bride (Esther) is finding the Lamp of David and filling it with fresh oil to "arise and shine" with the glory of the Father!

Finally, Hanukkah is a season for cleansing our temples so the blessings can flow. Haggai 2:10-23 is dated for Hanukkah and says: ***"from this day forward I will BLESS you!"***

It is time to operate as Esther did seated with the King! *"Arise, Shine for your light has come! And the glory of the LORD is risen upon you!"* (Isaiah 60:1)
