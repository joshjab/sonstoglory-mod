# HALLELUJAH!

by Paul Jablonowski

October 8, 2012

ALL THREE of the fall feasts (Trumpets, Atonement and Tabernacles) were very significant for me this year! Yahweh has brought breakthrough in many areas during these fall feasts in 2012, but here are the highlights of what He allowed me to witness and be a part of:

<img src="/images/newsletters/pictures/ark_of_covenant.JPG" data-vspace="8" data-hspace="8" data-align="left" width="480" height="360" />Pictured here is a life sized replica of the ark of the covenant. Every time my friend Diane tried to take a picture, it looked like it was in motion. This ark and the accompanying tabernacle were in Birmingham Alabama this year during the Feast of Trumpets (Rosh Hashanah) to participate in a city wide prayer and praise event called "Redigging the Wells." I first saw this ark of the covenant last year and wrote about it in my <a href="12worshipwarfare.htm" target="_blank">Worship Warfare</a> newsletter. The ark was the holiest item in the Tabernacle of Moses where the Shekinah Glory of God dwelt to give the nation of Israel direction and protection. Moses first created the ark of the covenant at Yahweh's direction to represent both His holy presence and His mercy.

I believe the LORD saw our sacrifice of praise and granted us His mercy for both Alabama and for the nation of Israel this year. The only banner that hung in the Tabernacle this year said, "HOLY ONE OF ISRAEL." This tabernacle and ark were placed in Kelly Ingram Park which is the Civil Rights park in Birmingham across the street from the Civil Rights museum that people come to from all over the world to visit. This prayer and praise event was intercession to ask God for mercy for the wrongs released 49 years ago on this anniversary when 4 young black children were killed at the hands of the KKK at the 16th Street Baptist church in 1963. The Tabernacle was set up for three days and nights of continuous praise and worship set to worship music. It was an awesome set apart time.

There was also a 25 minute public march in the streets of Birmingham from Kelly Ingram Park to Railroad Park singing praises to Jesus as our Bridegroom! An amazing <a href="/images/newsletters/pictures/King_Jesus_in_the_Clouds_as_Bridegroom.JPG" target="_blank">sign in the clouds picture</a> was taken by Brenda of the Lovelady Center during this processional which shows Jesus as the Bridegroom King!

**Stakes**: As a prophetic act of what the LORD was doing in the Spirit, I took four wooden stakes and wrote the entirety of Psalm 113 on each 8 inch stake. After prayer and declaration with several other intercessors, we planted one of these stakes in Huntsville AL and the other three in strategic locations around Birmingham. This was done today on the final eighth day of the Feast of Tabernacles which is the culmination of all the fall feasts of the LORD.

*"Look upon **Zion**, the city of our **appointed feasts**; Your eyes will see **Jerusalem**, a quiet home, A **tabernacle** that will not be taken down; Not one of its **STAKES** will ever be removed, Nor will any of its cords be broken."* Isaiah 33:20

The word Hallelujah is a Hebrew word meaning "Praise the LORD." Specifically "hallel" means praise, and "Jah" or "Yah" is the shortened version of the name of God or Yahweh. **Praise Yahweh** is one of the most precious expressions in all of the Word of God. It is especially meaningful when singing it from your heart to the LORD. We as His people are like his "stakes" that He is planting in the earth to declare His praise and His glory. We are planted even in the midst of overly controlling governments, and corrupt systems of religion such as sun worship. The answer to overcoming this is to establish praise and worship centers that will sing the praises of Jesus! Hallelujah for those 24/7 worship houses that are being raised up in our country and around the world!
